About ACM-SC

About Our Chapter

We are the ACM Student Chapter at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Amaravati Campus, established on 1st July 2023. Our team is a dedicated group of ACM members committed to fostering a vibrant community of computing enthusiasts within our university. We believe in the power of collaboration and innovation, and we are excited to shape the future of computing at our campus and beyond.

Membership Benefits

Networking

We provide opportunities to network and build relationships with like-minded individuals who share a passion for technology , which can help students connect with employers or mentors in their field.
Collaborative

ACM provides a collaborative environment where you can work with other like-minded individuals, share ideas, and learn from each other. This can be a great way to build your teamwork and leadership skills.
Skill Enhancement

As a member of ACM, you can choose from a variety of technical SIGs to enhance your technical skills in your area of interest, where you can engage in hands-on workshops, hackathons, coding challenges, etc.
Leadership

As an ACM member, you'll develop valuable skills like team management, event organization, volunteerism, and budgeting.
Hands-on learning

At ACM, we offer various opportunities to develop your technical skills in a practical way through workshops, hackathons, coding competitions and more.
Internships

Write your research paper under the guidance of a skilled mentor and boost your resume with excellent internship opportunities at ACM.
